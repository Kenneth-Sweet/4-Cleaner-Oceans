About 4 Cleaner Oceans

4 Cleaner Oceans became a non-profit organization in 2013 in Jekyll Island, a popular beach in South Glynn County, Georgia. This area has a critical barrier island ecosystem that the community greatly wanted to preserve. In 2016, 4 Cleaner Oceans expanded its projects to include beach cleanups throughout Georgia and the Carolinas. With over 65 beach cleanup projects a year, the organization has outgrown its simple paper and spreadsheet organizational system and seeks the creation of a more sophisticated database management system to keep track of its projects, donors, and volunteers. This will allow the organization to eventually expand its reach and increase the number of projects, and with it, the health of our oceans.
